THUGVIEW

Thug is an experiment in our fast growing decentralised community. The approach on which the thug is built based on the cat meme, we love dogecoin we love cats too, to do the justice thugkat came into existence. We believe the future of decentralised currency are the meme coins because of their united support from the public and its extremely fast adoption.

The quantity of ThugKat coins are limited with the approach of burning them quarterly in large chunks and throwing away 50% of the thug coin in an unknown wallet of which keys will be thrown. It is an ERC20 token which is based on the ethereum block chain.
